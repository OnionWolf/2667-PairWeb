Mount Royal University

crisis resources

Here at Mount Royal, we want you to feel safe and supported. If you or someone you know is going through a crisis or emergency, you can be prepared with the resources below.

24-hour Emergency Contact

Call 911

If there is an immediate danger to the health or safety of yourself or others call 911

If you are on campus, after calling 911, call MRU Security Services at 403.440.5900 or use a Red Phone. Security Services responds to alarms and accidents, provides emergency first aid, and responds to unusual activity on campus 24/7.

If the situation is health related and urgent, you can go to the nearest Hospital Emergency Department or Urgent Care Centre (e.g. Sheldon Chumir Centre).

Daytime on Campus Crisis Contact

Call Health Services and inform the reception that you are in crisis. A mental health nurse will connect with you to determine your health needs and help identify on- and off-campus crisis resources. This service is available to MRU students and employees.

MRU Health Services Clinic

Call 403.440.6326 and inform the reception that you are in crisis

24-hour on Campus Crisis Contacts

Concerning Behaviour on Campus Team (CBCT)

Call to report behaviour that could indicate a person may cause harm to themselves or others. Call 403.440.5900 and ask for the CBCT team

MRU Residence Advisor

For after-hours residence crisis support, call the Residence Advisor On Duty and ask to speak with a Residence Life Coordinator. If you live in MRU Residence, a member of the Residence Life team is available to provide you with support 24/7:

  • West RA On Duty (A & B Building) - 403.542.8215
  • West RA On Duty (C & D Building, West Townhouses) - 403.542.4738
  • East RA On Duty - 403.542.7258
