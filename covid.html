COVID-19

Wellness Services is continuing to monitor and respond to information and advisories from Alberta Health-related to COVID-19. For more information on COVID-19 and MRU's response, please visit our COVID-19 page.

Student Counselling Services is conducting appointments via telephone or video during COVID-19.

Masks or face coverings are mandatory

Anyone coming to campus must wear a mask or face covering at all times when others are present (in private and public spaces), unless alone in a room where a door can be closed.

This applies to students, employees, contractors, and visitors.

Wear a mask or face covering outdoors if you cannot maintain a 2-metre distance from others.

Appointments

We are committed to providing students with ongoing care and are offering telephone and video sessions. To discuss your options or to book, change, or cancel an appointment, please call 403.440.6362 or email counselling@mtroyal.ca between Monday and Friday from 9:00 am to 4:00 pm. We will respond to you within 48 business hours. Please be sure to include your name and a call-back number so that an administrative assistant can call you back.

Please note that we offer services adapted to current circumstances and there may be a delay in receiving and sending emails. If you are experiencing an emergency, call 911. If you need help now, please call 403.266.HELP (4357), which you can access 24 hours a day seven days a week, or access other off-campus resources with extended hours.

Support Information

If you are a student looking for information about COVID-19, please ensure you have reviewed the information related to MRU, including MRU's People Plan. In the People Plan document, Appendix D outlines the Rapid Response Plan, which includes the process for symptomatic students. If you have questions, are unsure about processes for MRU students related to COVID-19, or require additional support, you can contact Health Services at 403.440.6326.

Here are some resources to support your wellbeing during this difficult time:

  • Visit mru.ca/MentalHealth for information about on and off campus resources
  • Information about bi-weekly student support webinars from Student Counselling Services focused on coping, resilience, and resources
  • Tips on healthy eating during COVID-19 from Alberta Health Services
  • Information about COVID-19 in Canada from the Government of Canada
  • Text4Hope offered by Alberta Health Services for daily messages focused on advice and encouragement in developing healthy personal coping skills and resiliency during the pandemic
  • Financial Aid Information for Students from the Government of Alberta
